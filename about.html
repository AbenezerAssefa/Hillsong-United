All About The Event

We’re so excited to announce that the Hillsong Worship tour is coming to Ethiopia! As the Hillsong team joins together in Ethiopia for an unforgettable evening of powerful worship celebration, our passion is for everyone to experience God in a whole new way. Get ready for a night that will move your spirit, refresh your heart and take you on a journey as we come into His presence together. A night where we are all united in worshipping our King! Global ministries and YWAM will also be joining us on tour around Ethiopia to spread God’s love through acts of service. So don’t miss out - join us today!
